HSL picker (JavaScript)

Click the color-wheel to pick a Hue from 0 to 360 (going counter-clock-wise). Drag the corresponding sliders to pick the Saturation and Lightness (from 0% to 100%). You'll see the HSL numeric values and the sample color, in the rectangle below.
